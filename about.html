About me. 

I'm a freelance web designer and Webflow developer based in Zagreb, Croatia. With a passion for clean and functional design, I help small businesses and individuals bring their online presence to life.

As a Webflow developer, I bring a unique skill set to the table. Webflow allows me to build custom, responsive websites without writing a single line of code. This results in fast, efficient, and cost-effective projects for my clients.

When I'm not designing websites, I enjoy exploring the latest web design trends and techniques, learning new technologies, and finding new ways to improve my skills.

If you're looking for a reliable web designer to bring your vision to life, I'd love to hear from you. Let's work together to create a website that truly represents your brand and captures the essence of your business.
